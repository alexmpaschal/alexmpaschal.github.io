I'm a third-year mathematics student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I research ergodic theory (in particular, thermodynamic formalism) under [Daniel Thompson](https://people.math.osu.edu/thompson.2455/index.html). I'm currently abroad at École normale supérieure de Lyon, studying PDE in their [MA2 program](https://mathematiques.ens-lyon.fr/master/master-2/2025-2026-program) on an SFRI MathInfi scholarship. This summer I'm researching optimal transport (in particular, gradient flows (in particular, the JKO scheme)) under [Filippo Santambrogio](https://math.univ-lyon1.fr/~santambrogio/). Next year I'll be the president of the [Carolina Math Club](https://heellife.unc.edu/organization/carolinamathclub).
